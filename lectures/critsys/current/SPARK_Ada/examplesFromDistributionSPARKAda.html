Examples in the Distribution of SPARK Ada

  • Examples in the distribution
    • In the distribution there are examples of SPARK Ada programs located at depending where it was installed initially.
    • In a Linux installation typical places are (note you need to navigate to this place starting from Root)
      /usr/gnat/share/examples/spark/

      or

      /opt/spark2014/share/examples/spark/

      • Examples involving IO are located in
        /usr/gnat/share/examples/spark/spark_io/

        or

        /opt/spark2014/share/examples/spark/spark_io/
